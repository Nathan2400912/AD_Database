Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using the database

General Questions

What types of data can I extract from this database?

You can retrieve:

  • Gene expression profiles across conditions
  • CRE genomic coordinates and activity states
  • TF binding interactions and networks
  • Pathway associations for genes
  • Disease-specific regulatory relationships
How current is the data in this database?

We regularly update with:

  • Quarterly updates from published studies
  • New ATAC-seq/CUT&Tag data from collaborators
  • Monthly TF interaction updates from STRING DB

Technical Questions

How are CRE-gene interactions determined?

We use three complementary methods:

  1. Physical proximity (within 500kb of TSS)
  2. Hi-C chromatin interaction data
  3. TF-mediated co-regulation evidence
